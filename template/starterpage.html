Workshop at Hasoub Garage


At Hasoub Garage, we are committed to providing exceptional learning experiences through our tailored lectures and workshops. These sessions cover a broad range of topics, including professional development, soft skills, and advanced technologies such as 3D modeling.



Booking a Lecture


  • Submit Your Request: To arrange a lecture or workshop for your group, please fill out the inquiry form below with your preferred date, topics of interest, and any other relevant details.

  • Pricing Information: After receiving your request, we will provide you with the applicable fees for the session.



Renting A Space at Hasoub Garage


Hasoub Garage offers its collaborative and private spaces for rent, perfect for tech projects, workshops, team meetings, and more. Our spaces are designed to support productivity and innovation.



How to Rent Our Space


  • Submit Your Request: Fill out the form below with your details, including the type of event or project, desired rental dates, and any specific requirements.

  • Discuss Details: Once we receive your request, we will contact you to discuss availability and finalize the rental terms.
